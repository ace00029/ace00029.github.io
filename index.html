Welcome to Total Trip

Posted by Total Trip Team on November 01, 2022

Are you willing to travel? Everyone could plan a vacation nowadays. However, taking the great amount of time to navigate all through the web and plan every single detail is not in everyone's hand. In order to make the trip planning experience more pleasant for the customer, our website project offers helpful filtering like real-time weather service, recommendations about the destination based on our algorithm that chooses among different users rating, places to eat traditional foods choosing the best price and user evaluation, the best ways of transport to get around, where to accommodate and earlier user experiences allocated in the specifically designed database, all in one, so there is no need any more to worry about planning your vacations, it will all be done for you!
